Overview
Our college consists of the Department of Social Sciences, the Department of physical education, the Department of basic education, the school of nursing, the school of clinical medicine, the school of medicine, the school of medical technology, the Department of Stomatology, the Department of optometry and the Department of hotel management. There are 17 majors including clinical medicine, Stomatology, nursing, midwifery, pharmacy, Chinese pharmacy, medical inspection technology, medical biotechnology, medical imaging technology, stomatology technology, health inspection and quarantine technology, optometry technology, rehabilitation treatment technology, health information management, medical nutrition, accounting, hotel management, etc. We have cooperated with more than 70 stable third class a internship hospitals and more than 200 internship training bases, providing good conditions for students' clinical internship and practice. We maintain good cooperative relations with the United States, Britain, Japan and other countries, as well as Taiwan and Hong Kong. The college attaches great importance to the cultivation of students' professional quality and strengthens practical teaching. Graduates with good attitude, fast adaptation and strong ability are widely welcomed in the society. The employment rate has always been kept above 98%, and the quality of employment is good.
